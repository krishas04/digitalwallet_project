Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I add money to my wallet?

You can add money easily from the dashboard. Click on the "Load Money" service, enter the amount you wish to add, and follow the on-screen instructions to complete the payment from your linked bank account or card.

Is my money safe with WebPay?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your account and transactions. Your financial data is never stored on our servers, and we monitor for suspicious activity 24/7.

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, you can reset it by clicking the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. We will send a secure link to your registered email address to help you set a new one.

What should I do if a transaction fails?

First, check your Transaction History to see the status. If a transaction has failed, any deducted amount will be automatically refunded to your account within 3-5 business days. If the issue persists, please contact our support team with the transaction ID.
